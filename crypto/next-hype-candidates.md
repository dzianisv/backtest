# The Next HYPE — Candidate Hunt

> Deep research, 2026-06-05. **Educational analysis, not financial advice.** Crypto book = separate
> track. Method: apply the 6-point infra-token filter (see `crypto/InfraTokens.md`) to hunt EARLY
> tokens with HYPE's winning DNA. Three parallel screens: perps/DEX vertical, emerging verticals,
> quant cashflow screen.

## The filter (HYPE's DNA)
1. Organic, **growing** fee revenue (not incentivized/wash). 2. **Enforced** fee→token accrual
(auto buyback/burn). 3. Category leadership/moat. 4. **Beats BTC** (BTC-denominated uptrend).
5. Clean FDV / low forward dilution. 6. Durable through a bear (not reflexive).

## ⚠️ The honest meta-finding
The intersection **cheap + real cashflow + enforced accrual + low dilution + BEATING BTC is nearly
empty.** Filter #4 (BTC-relative strength) is the killer — almost every real-cashflow name is *cheap
but losing to BTC* (value trap unless the trend turns). **Lighter is the only NEW name that is both
fundamentally real AND beating BTC.** This re-confirms the thesis: most "fundamental" alts still bleed
vs BTC. None of these replace a BTC core; size them as asymmetric satellites you may lose vs BTC.

- Vertical: perp DEX. **zk-proven price-time priority** = structurally wash-resistant (the opposite of Aster's problem).
- Revenue **re-accelerating**: ~$75k → **$211k/day** (late May→early Jun) ≈ $53–77M annualized and climbing.
- **Enforced-ish buyback** live since Jan 2026: ~7.48M LIT / ~$18M bought ($14.6M in Q1).
- **Token +99% vs BTC/30d** ✅ (the only candidate passing the BTC-strength filter).
- Mcap ~$378M, mcap/FDV ~0.19–0.25.
- **Filters:** 1 PASS* (revenue re-accelerating off a post-incentive washout), 2 ~PASS, 3 PASS (top-4 perp DEX), 4 **PASS**, 5 PARTIAL, 6 UNPROVEN.
- **Biggest risk:** revenue re-accel still partly incentive-tied; **team+investor unlock cliff starts Dec 2026** — if revenue stalls before then, dilution hits into weakness.
- **Action:** small starter only; verify revenue stays organic; respect the Dec-2026 cliff.

### 🥈 2. SYRUP (Maple Finance) — best clean-tokenomics small cap
- Vertical: RWA / on-chain institutional credit. AUM ~10× YoY to ~$4B.
- **Hardcoded 25%-of-revenue buyback** (MIP-019, enforced, non-discretionary).
- Mcap ~$184M, **~fully circulating = no unlock overhang** (cleanest tokenomics here).
- **Filters:** 1 PASS (growing), 2 PASS (enforced), 3 PARTIAL (credit is competitive), 4 **FAIL** (lagging BTC), 5 PASS, 6 PARTIAL.

- **Action:** best risk-adjusted small-cap; either wait for a BTC-relative turn or hold as a value bet sized for the credit tail.
