# Crypto Research & Decision — GOAL (1-min read)

> Educational analysis only — not financial advice. Recommend-only, notification-first, human-in-the-loop.
> **This is the RESEARCH/DECISION product** — the panel + workflow + skills that answer "should I buy/sell/hold BTC (or an alt) now, and how much?" The sibling [GOAL.md](GOAL.md) is the separate DeFi *yield* book (~$177k portfolio). Two books, two goals — do not conflate.

## The mission

Produce **disciplined, evidence-grounded crypto deploy decisions** — never a hot take, never a single lens.
Every call rests on a consolidated brief that fuses **all material, price-affecting inputs** and is then
debated by a multi-lens panel, scored later for calibration.

## The honest edge (what we are and aren't)

The agent has **no prediction edge** — anything cheap and scalable is already priced ([[scalable-alpha-mirage]]).
The edge is **judgment, discipline, validation**:
- **Completeness** — never miss a market-moving input (price, positioning, macro, liquidity, **news catalysts**).
- **Discipline** — staged tranches over conviction lump-sums; sizing survivable to a further −50%.
- **Validation** — every dated call logged and Brier-scored, so the panel is accountable to reality.
- **Honest disagreement** — preserve the bear dissent; never average it away.

## Non-negotiables

1. **Recommend-only.** Never custody keys, sign, or auto-trade. The human executes.
2. **No fabricated data.** Mark `[UNAVAILABLE]`; never invent a number or an odds figure (always pull priced
   probabilities live via [[prediction-market-odds]], never from a news digest).
3. **Capital boundary.** Crypto risk comes only from the ~$0.5M risk capital, never the $1.5M house reserve.
4. **Completeness is a hard goal, not a nice-to-have.** A material catalyst (e.g. a large corporate-treasury
   BTC buy, an ETF-flow swing, a regulation or exploit) **must** surface in the brief. Missing one is a defect.

## Definition of done (per decision)

A dated `research.crypto.{date}.md` with: sourced evidence brief (incl. a **news/narrative section**), panel
votes + preserved disagreement, a chair decision with a concrete tranche plan + invalidation, and the call
logged to the forecast ledger.
