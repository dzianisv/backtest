## Books (primary)
- **Trade Wars Are Class Wars: How Rising Inequality Distorts the Global Economy and Threatens International Peace** — Matthew C. Klein & Michael Pettis, Yale University Press, 2020. The flagship statement of capital-flows-drive-trade + inequality/underconsumption. https://yalebooks.yale.edu/book/9780300261448/trade-wars-are-class-wars/ (search-only; publisher page).
- **The Great Rebalancing: Trade, Conflict, and the Perilous Road Ahead for the World Economy** — Michael Pettis, Princeton UP, 2013. Savings identities, China/Germany/US imbalances, dollar burden. https://www.amazon.com/Great-Rebalancing-Conflict-Perilous-Economy/dp/1491531339 (search-only).
- **Avoiding the Fall: China's Economic Restructuring** — Michael Pettis, Carnegie, 2013. The three transfers from households to producers; rebalancing paths. https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/18564223-avoiding-the-fall (search-only).
- **The Volatility Machine: Emerging Economies and the Threat of Financial Collapse** — Michael Pettis, Oxford UP, 2001. Balance-sheet/liability-structure crisis framework (foundational, not separately fetched).
