## Reachability / provenance notes
- Graham's two books are in print and widely quoted; chapter content is verified against the standard editions (TII 4th rev. ed. 1973 / 2003 Collins; *Security Analysis* 1940 2nd ed.). Page numbers vary by edition, so references cite **chapters**, not pages.
- The **1976 FAJ interview** is the single time-sensitive primary source and the basis for `05-applying-graham-today.md`'s "his own last word" section; it is freely available via CFA Institute / FAJ archives.
- The **Graham number** √(22.5 × EPS × BVPS) is a later formalization (textbook/practitioner) of Graham's ch. 14 combined P/E×P/B ≤ 22.5 rule — attributed as such, not as Graham's own published formula.
- Net-net extinction and the 2007–2020 value drawdown (`05`) are sourced to the academic value-factor literature (Fama–French HML series) and practitioner net-net studies — **re-check these regime claims against current data** before quoting.
