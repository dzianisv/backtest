## Book (primary)
- "Anatomy of the Bear: Lessons from Wall Street's Four Great Bottoms" — Russell Napier — Harriman House, 1st ed. 2005, 4th ed. 2016. https://www.harriman-house.com/anatomy4th — Reachable (publisher page); book text PAYWALLED (purchase). The four great bottoms (1921/1932/1949/1982), Tobin's Q ≈ 0.3, CAPE 4.7-11.7, "deflation kills equity."
  - Summary: ValueWalk — https://www.valuewalk.com/anatomy-of-the-bear/ — Reachable. Q ratio 70% discount, summer bottoms, society-shift eras.
  - Annotated: RealInvestmentAdvice "Bear Market Anatomy – Revisiting Napier's Work" — https://realinvestmentadvice.com/resources/blog/bear-market-anatomy-revisiting-russell-napiers-work-annotated/ — 404 (moved).   - MoneyWeek "Russell Napier: S&P 500 'will fall 70%'" — https://moneyweek.com/288780/russell-napier-sp-500-will-fall-70 — Reachable. His deflationist bottom-call (evidence of the early/wrong period).

## Newsletter (primary)
- "The Solid Ground" — Russell Napier's global macro strategy report; begun 1995 (originally for CLSA). Distributed via the Electronic Research Interchange (ERIC). https://www.eri-c.com — Reachable; content PAYWALLED (subscription). Source of the financial-repression / structural-inflation thesis.
- Library of Mistakes — Napier's financial-history library/charity project, Edinburgh. https://www.libraryofmistakes.com — Reachable. Context for his historical method.
