## How the Scoring Works

| Signal | Why it matters |
|--------|---------------|
| Board seats demanded | Forces change; highest catalyst |
| Repeat activist filer | Knows how to run a campaign |
| Stake ≥10% | Can't walk away — locked in |
| Deep discount (−30%+ from 52w) | More room for re-rating |
| Filed <7 days ago | Fresh = not yet priced in |
| Multi-filer convergence | Multiple independent actors = strongest structural signal |
| Activist letter published | Public pressure accelerates timeline |

None of these are buy signals on their own — they're hypotheses that multi-lens-quorum validates or rejects by looking at fundamentals, valuation, liquidity, and whether the business is actually fixable.
