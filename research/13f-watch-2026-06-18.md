## Methodology & Limitations

### What this report IS
- A 45-day-lagged snapshot of US-listed long equity positions > $100M (13F threshold)
- A directional signal for which companies attracted super-investor capital as of March 31, 2026
- Hypothesis generation for further research (quorum, backtest) — NOT a trade signal

### What this report is NOT
- Real-time — managers may have already exited by now (45+ day lag)
- Complete — 13F does not show shorts, puts (unless long puts), non-US positions, positions < $100M, or private investments
- A buy signal — "Buffett holds X" is not DD; it's a starting point
